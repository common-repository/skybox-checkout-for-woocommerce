Skybox Checkout lets WooCommerce stores sell Internationally. Fully landed cost, logistics and payment solution.

== Description ==

International Checkout Solution allows you to instantly sell to 200 countries & territories and accept payment in local currencies while removing all risk for fraud and eliminating additional fees upon delivery to your international customers.

<br>

= Features =

<p></p>
<ul>
    <li>100% Price Guarantee no additional fees collected upon international delivery. Orders go direct to your customers door.</li>
    <li>Multilingual Customer Service support available by phone, chat and email.</li>
    <li>No risk and no disruption to your current operations. Simply ship to one of our conveniently located global processing hubs in LAX, MIA or NYC.</li>
    <li>Regional Marketing Tools for local pricing and promotions.</li>
    <li>Dedicated Cross Border eCommerce Account Manager for ongoing global expansion planning and support.</li>
    <li>Online Merchant and Customer Portals for complete access, updates and visibility on all international orders.</li>
    <li>Customizable International Returns Workflows and Logistics Support.</li>

</ul>
